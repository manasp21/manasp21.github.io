Hello, I'm Manas Pandey

Undergraduate at IIT Kanpur, exploring the fascinating intersection of Physics and Artificial Intelligence.

My gateway to understanding reality has always been physics. I'm constantly amazed by how elegant math, from the quantum level to machine learning algorithms, can so effectively model the complex systems we observe and test. What really drives me is seeing these principles in action through technology and innovation.

I thrive on getting my hands dirty, whether it's diving into the latest AI research, debating new experimental results in physics, or collaborating on computational projects that involve real-world data. For me, the most exciting environments are those where we can build things, test ideas, and watch knowledge grow from our collective effort.

Outside the lab, I find a similar sense of beauty in music, poetry, and art. The patterns and structures in these creative fields often feel connected to the phenomena I study in the lab, creating a cool feedback loop between analytical work and aesthetic inspiration that keeps my work fresh and exciting.
